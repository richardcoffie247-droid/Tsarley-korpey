Activities & Experiences

Life on the Volta

01

Volta Estuary Boat Cruises

Glide across the still waters at dawn or dusk. Our guided cruises take you through the estuary where birdlife thrives and the light turns gold.

02

Kayaking & Water Sports

Paddle where the Volta meets the Atlantic. Kayaks, jet skis, and sailing are available for guests at the private dock.

03

Dangme Cultural Immersion

Explore the rich heritage of Ada Foah — kente weaving, drumming sessions, and guided walks through historic fishing communities.

04

Local Market Excursions

The Ada Foah market bursts with colour every Wednesday and Saturday. A short journey through the heart of local life.

05

Tilapia & Jollof Dining

Our open-air restaurant serves fresh-caught tilapia, jollof, and banku — local recipes prepared with generational knowledge.

06

Marine Turtle Watching

Ada Foah's coast is a nesting ground for marine turtles. Seasonal guided night walks offer a rare, unforgettable encounter.
